## Dependencies & Ordering Notes

- **#2 (AI cost cap) and #29 (Sentry org tags) can be done together** — the org-tag work is half the value of the cost cap (observability without enforcement).
- **#3 (data minimisation) should land before #6 (parental consent)** — minimisation reduces what consent must cover.
- **#7 (Sentry filter) blocks #15 (audit-log reads)** — audit logs land in Sentry breadcrumbs unless filtered.
- **#10 (students-list optimisation) and #9 (cover batching) are independent** — can parallelise.
- **#12 (cross-tenant test) is independent of everything** — can be the first commit by anyone wanting an easy onramp.
