# Tally Reading — School Onboarding Guide

Welcome to Tally Reading! This guide will help your school get up and running.

---

## Part 1: IT Department Setup

Please forward this section to your IT team before your first login.

### Domain Whitelist

Tally Reading is a web-based application — no software installation is required. Your IT team needs to ensure the following domain is accessible from your school network:

| Domain | Protocol | Port |
|---|---|---|
| `tallyreading.uk` | HTTPS | 443 |

**If teachers see a "Your connection isn't private" error**, this means your school firewall or web filter is blocking the site. Ask your IT team to add `tallyreading.uk` to their allowed domains list.

### Browser Requirements

Tally Reading works in any modern browser. We recommend:
- Google Chrome (latest)
- Microsoft Edge (latest)
- Safari (latest)

No browser extensions or plugins are needed.

## Part 2: Getting Started

### Your Account

Your school's administrator account will be set up for you. You'll receive an email with your login details and a link to sign in.

On first login, you'll be prompted to change your password. Passwords must be at least 8 characters.

### Adding Teachers

1. Go to **Settings** from the sidebar
2. Select **Manage Users**
3. Click **Add User**
4. Enter the teacher's name, email, and select the **Teacher** role
5. They'll receive an email with login instructions

Teachers can manage their own students, classes, and reading sessions. If you need a colleague to help with admin tasks, assign them the **Admin** role instead.

### Creating Classes

1. Go to **Classes** from the sidebar
2. Click **Add Class**
3. Enter the class name (e.g. "Year 3 Oak")
4. Assign a teacher to the class

### Adding Students

1. Go to **Students** from the sidebar
2. Click **Add Student**
3. Enter the student's name and assign them to a class
4. Optionally set their reading level range (AR levels 1.0–13.0)

Students can also be added in bulk — see the section below on importing data.

## Part 3: Explore Features

Once your school is set up with classes and students, here's what you can do:

### Import Your Book Library

If you have an existing book list as a spreadsheet, you can import it via **Books → Import Books**. The import wizard accepts CSV files and will automatically match columns and detect duplicates.

### Record Reading Sessions

Teachers can log individual reading sessions from a student's profile, recording the book, pages read, and notes.

### Home Reading Register

For quick daily logging across an entire class, use the **Home Reading Register**. It provides a simple grid where teachers can mark each student's reading status for the day.

### Book Recommendations

Tally Reading can suggest books tailored to each student's reading level and interests. Access this from a student's profile under **Recommend Books**.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---|---|
| "Your connection isn't private" error | Ask your IT team to whitelist `tallyreading.uk` on the school firewall |
| Forgot your password | Click **Forgot Password** on the login screen to receive a reset email |
| A teacher can't see their class | Check they've been assigned to the class under **Classes → Edit** |
| Need help? | Contact us at **admin@tallyreading.uk** |
