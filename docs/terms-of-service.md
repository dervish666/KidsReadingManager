## 2. The Service

Tally Reading is a cloud-based reading management platform designed for UK
primary schools. The Service enables schools to:

- Track pupil reading progress and sessions
- Manage book libraries with barcode scanning and metadata lookup
- Generate AI-powered book recommendations (optional, requires API key)
- Record home reading via a class register
- Import pupil and class data via Wonde school integration
- Export data for reporting purposes

The Service is hosted on Cloudflare's edge infrastructure. Data is processed
in accordance with our Privacy Policy (https://tallyreading.uk/privacy) and,
where applicable, the Data Processing Agreement between us and your school.

## 5. AI book recommendations

### 5.1 Optional feature

AI-powered book recommendations are an optional feature. Schools must
provide their own API key from a supported AI provider (Anthropic, OpenAI,
or Google) to use this feature.

### 5.2 Data sent to AI providers

When recommendations are generated, the Service sends the following to the
configured AI provider:

- The pupil's reading level range and genre preferences
- A selection of books from the school's library (titles, authors, levels)
- The pupil's recent reading history (book titles only)

**No pupil names, dates of birth, or other identifying information are sent
to AI providers.** See our Privacy Policy and DPIA for full details.

### 5.3 AI provider terms

Use of AI recommendations is also subject to the terms of your chosen AI
provider. We are not responsible for the processing of data by third-party
AI providers once it leaves our platform.

## 6. Wonde and MyLogin integration

### 6.1 Data sync

Schools that use the Wonde integration authorise Tally to receive pupil,
class, and staff data from their Management Information System (MIS) via the
Wonde API. This sync is governed by the school's existing agreement with
Wonde and the DPA between the school and Tally.

### 6.2 Single sign-on

MyLogin SSO is provided by Wonde. When staff sign in via MyLogin, we receive
their name, email address, and role from MyLogin's OAuth2 profile. We do not
receive or store MyLogin passwords.
